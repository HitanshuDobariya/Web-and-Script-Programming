EcoSaver: Smart Energy Management System

"EcoSaver" is an innovative smart energy management system designed to promote energy efficiency and sustainability in residential and commercial settings. This project aims to address the growing demand for sustainable energy practices and reduce energy consumption while maximizing cost savings.

  • Real-time Energy Monitoring
  • Smart Appliance Integration
  • Energy Analytics
  • Energy Budgeting
  • Environmental Impact Calculation
  • Integration with Renewable Energy Sources
  • User-Friendly Interface
  • Smart Notifications
